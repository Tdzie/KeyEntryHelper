Welcome to KeyInHelper

This interactive lesson will guide you through the steps of entering in various types of products.

First, study this example of how to enter in a normal UPC.

Click image to enlarge!

Throughout the lessons, you will be asked to interact with the register on the left to complete the steps.

Try it out! Using your mouse, use the number pad on the right side of the register to add digits to the display, then click ENTER to run the lookup.

Try entering the UPC from the barcode in the above picture.

If a incorrect barcode is entered the register will display a Not Found box and the number pad will lock, press C to clear the error and start again.

When you are ready, click the Continue button on the bottom right to start.
